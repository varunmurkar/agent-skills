# Responsive Checklist

- Mobile-first layout works without horizontal scrolling.
- Touch targets are comfortably tappable.
- Typography remains readable across breakpoints.
- Essential actions remain visible on narrow viewports.
- Test at least one narrow and one wide viewport.

## Common Pitfalls

- Fixed widths/heights without content resilience.
- Hiding critical actions in non-obvious overflow surfaces.
- Breakpoint-only design without real-content testing.
